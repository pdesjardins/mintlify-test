### Partners

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Partner access" icon="handshake">
    Do you have a business agreement with Toast? Are you integrating your product with the Toast platform?

    **Next steps**

    You are an external technology company that extends the Toast platform using Toast APIs. Your API access is granted through the Toast Partner Integrations program, which includes a formal application and certification process. Once approved, you receive scoped API access that enables you to read and write data on behalf of shared customers.
