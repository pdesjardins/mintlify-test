The enterprise module, also known as multi-location management, is used by Toast customers that have multiple restaurants. It allows you to create common configurations in Toast Web that can be shared by your restaurant locations. For example, your restaurants can share menus (or portions of menus), void reasons, employees and job descriptions, and so on. The more configuration you share among your locations, the easier they become to maintain because you are managing one instance of a shared entity used by multiple locations, as opposed to many instances for each specific location. For settings that are not shared among locations, there is the option of copying settings from one location to another. For more information, see [Settings copy overview](platformSettingsCopyOverview.html).

The enterprise module must be enabled by Toast support before you can use it. The multi-location management is included with [Restaurant Management Pro](https://www.toasttab.com/customers/shop/software-services/restaurant-management-suite?tier=Pro).



> **Note**
> 
> This section uses the terms "restaurant" and "location" interchangeably.
