When you redeem credits as payment for a check, the Toast platform applies the value of the credit as a discount.

Toast Web Analytics & Reports \> Overview report includes the value of redeemed guest credits in the Discount Amount column and in the order details display. The Analytics & Reports \> Finance Overview includes customer credit redemptions on the discounts report.
