You can view your active and archived house accounts in Toast Web. Go to Payments &gt; Payment methods &gt; House accounts to open the Accounts page. On the Accounts page, you can view the following information:

- Total number of house accounts


- Total outstanding balance of house accounts


- List of active and archived house accounts



On the Accounts page, you can complete the following actions:

- Choose to create either a single or multiple house accounts


- Search for a house account by name or account number


- Use the date picker to search for active house accounts with transactions created within a time range



> **Note**
> 
> If a time range is selected, this populates the Total Spend column. The total spend is the total amount charged to a house account and the total amount invoiced to the account. For more information, see [Paying for orders with a house account](docs/en-us/adminGuide-platformEnableHouseAccountsPaymentMethod#platformPayWithHouseAccounts).



- Filter house accounts by outstanding balance or last transaction date


- Show and hide various table column options


- Download house accounts table details
