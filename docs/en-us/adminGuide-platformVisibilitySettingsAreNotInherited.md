Menu entities do not inherit their visibility settings from their
    parents. For example, a menu item does not inherit the visibility settings
    that are configured for its parent menu group. However, if you hide a menu
    entity from a particular channel, all of its children are hidden as well.
    To continue the earlier example, if you hide a menu group from a channel,
    then any menu items in that group are also hidden from that
    channel.
