To view information about service charges that were applied to orders, you can use the **Sales Summary** report or the **Order Details** report.

The **Sales Summary** report lists the service charge names and the amounts that were applied to orders.

To view the **Sales Summary** report:

1. Choose **Reports &gt; Sales &gt; Sales summary**.


2. Use the **View** options at the top of the page to select a period of time, daily time frame, set of employees, and restaurant location.


3. Click the **Load Report** button.



The **Order Details** report lists the service charges (if any) that were applied to each order. It also lists the service charge details, such as the tax and whether the service charge is a gratuity.

To view the **Order Details** report, choose **Reports &gt; Menus &gt; Order details**.
