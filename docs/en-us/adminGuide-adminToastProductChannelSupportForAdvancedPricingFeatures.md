It is important to note that not all advanced pricing features are
    supported on all Toast product channels. For example, Toast Online
    Ordering does not support time-specific or open prices so you should not
    use those pricing strategies for menu items that will be displayed on your
    Toast Online Ordering website. Before using an advanced pricing feature,
    check the table below to ensure that the strategy you want to use is
    supported by the Toast product channels where those menu items will be
    visible.

| [Size Price](adminGuide-adminSizePrice) pricing
            strategy | For menu items and menu groups, size-specific prices
            are supported on:- Toast POS app
- Toast Online Ordering
- Toast Takeout (Local by Toast) app
- Toast Kiosk
- Toast Order and Pay
- Online ordering partners (Support can vary by online
                  ordering partner, contact partner to confirm.)

For modifier groups and modifiers,
            size-specific prices are supported on:- Toast POS app
- Toast Online Ordering
- Toast Kiosk
- Online ordering partners (Support can vary by online
                  ordering partner, contact partner to confirm.)
