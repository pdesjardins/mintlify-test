### Pre-shift notes overview

The pre-shift notes feature allows restaurants to keep their staff informed of key announcements, reminders, and updates during their shift.

Benefits to the pre-shift notes feature include:

- Providing a dedicated channel for management to communicate with staff during their shift.


- Giving managers an easy way to write, prioritize, and highlight important information for staff after they clock into their shift.


- Providing a consolidated view of menu item stock statuses.
