### Payment workflow overview

The Toast POS platform supports multiple ways for restaurant guests to make payments. The following sections describe the available payment methods, and how each method is used, on a Toast POS device.

The following diagram shows the payment workflow for standard Toast POS payment types. The payment workflow for your restaurant might be different, based on your restaurant's configuration and custom payment types.



> **Note**
> 
> This diagram does not include all custom payment types. Your restaurant’s payment workflow may be different based on restaurant configuration.
