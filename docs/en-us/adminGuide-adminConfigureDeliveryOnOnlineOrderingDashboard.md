The [online ordering dashboard](adminGuide-adminOnlineOrderingDashboard)
    displays delivery settings, such as your delivery quote time and
    applicable throttling delays. From the dashboard, you can turn delivery on
    or off or add a delay.

For first-party delivery orders, the dashboard displays the delivery
    quote time. For TDS orders, the dashboard displays the takeout quote time
    plus the delivery time. For example, if a guest places an order at 2:00 PM
    with a 30 minute quote time and a 30 minute delivery time, the expected
    delivery time for that order is 3:00 PM.



> **Note**
> 
> The delivery time is dynamic, meaning it changes based on the time
      of day, day of the week, guest location, and other criteria.
