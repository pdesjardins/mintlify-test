You can create stock refresh schedules that automatically refresh a menu item or modifier's stock status and quantity (count) on a defined schedule.

You can set the menu item or modifier so that its stock status refreshes to **In stock** or to a specific stock quantity. If your organization has multiple locations, all stock refresh updates are made in each location's local time.

For detailed information, see [Refreshing a menu item or modifier's stock automatically](adminGuide-platformMenuManagerWorkingWithMenuItems#platformRefreshingAMenuItemsStockAutomatically).
