### Visibility overview

You use visibility settings to determine the channels where a menu entity is visible and available for ordering. A channel is a device, an app, or a website where guests and servers can view menus and place orders. So, for example, these settings control whether an entity can be ordered on a Toast POS device, on a Toast Kiosk, via a restaurant's Toast Online Ordering website, or via a restaurant's integration with a third-party online ordering system.
