When the Autofire device goes offline, approval for online orders and automatic firing of scheduled orders is paused until a new device is set as the Autofire device. The Toast platform does not automatically reassign another device to Autofire. To change your Autofire device, see [Configuring an autofire device](docs/en-us/adminGuide-platformConfiguringAutoFireDevice).
