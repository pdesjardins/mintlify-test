After completing a pricing change, be sure to save it. Whether or not you also publish your change depends on whether you have completed the price configuration process. Because published prices are immediately available for use on the Toast POS app, Toast support recommends that you wait to publish your prices until you have gone through the initial process of defining prices for all your menu entities.

For information about how to publish configuration changes, see [Publishing updates to restaurant configuration](docs/en-us/adminGuide-platformPublishingOverview).
