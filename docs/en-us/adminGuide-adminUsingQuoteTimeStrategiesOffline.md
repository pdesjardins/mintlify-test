### Using quote time strategies offline

If your Toast device is offline due to a network connectivity issue, only the SmartQuote strategy is affected. SmartQuote defaults to the last enabled quote time strategy. For example, if you enable the Manual quote time strategy for 20 minutes for breakfast, but switch to SmartQuote at lunch time, and then experience a network outage, your quote time defaults back to the Manual quote time of 20 minutes.
